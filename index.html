Ninja H2R

Kawasaki Ninja H2R is a super bike available in only 1 variant and 1 colour. Ninja H2R price starts at Rs. 79,90,000 in India. The Kawasaki Ninja H2R is powered by 998cc BS6 engine.

BMW G 310 RR
